PT3S¶

Use SIR 3S Modeldata and SIR 3S Results in pure Python.

With pandas, matplotlib and others.

For documentation, test, verification, analysis, reporting, prototyping, play.

Install¶

Python 3.5.2 (Anaconda 4.2.0 (64-bit))
